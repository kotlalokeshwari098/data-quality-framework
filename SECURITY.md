## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of this project seriously and appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose vulnerabilities.

### What to Include

When reporting a vulnerability, please provide as much detail as possible, including:

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce (if available)
- Any relevant logs, system details, or screenshots
- Potential impact of the issue

### Response Process

- You will receive an acknowledgment of your report within **5 business days**.
- We will investigate and provide status updates at least **every 10 business days**.
- Once verified, we will work to fix the issue as quickly as possible and may request additional information.
- After resolution, we will coordinate disclosure following responsible reporting practices.

We kindly request that you **do not publicly disclose** the vulnerability before we have released a fix.

Thank you for helping us keep this project secure.
